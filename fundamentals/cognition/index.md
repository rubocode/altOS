## Stimulus, Cognition &amp; Response

> I think, therefore, I am!  
> **Ren&eacute; Descartes**

When speak of a mechanism — human or machine — capable of intelligence, it is easy to speak of him, her or it altogether as an **agent**.

An agent has behavior in response to triggers — or stimulus — and we infer intelligence from the way the agent responds to the stimulus.
