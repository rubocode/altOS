## Application

> Leadership is solving problems.
> The day soldiers stop bringing
> you their problems is the day
> you have stopped leading them.  
> **Colin Powell**

You need to apply the knowledge you gather in order to ascertain the
validity of that knowledge.  Unproven knowledge is just unverified information.
We call this the ***application*** or the ***execution*** phase.
